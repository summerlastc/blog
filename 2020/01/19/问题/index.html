    1. centos7下yum升级被PackageKit锁定

    centos7下yum升级被PackageKit锁定

    (1)  centos7下，打开： /etc/yum/pluginconf.d/langpacks.conf

    (2) 将第一行：enable=1改为enable=0

    (3) 重启centos7，执行sudo yum update
